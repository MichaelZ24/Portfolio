Runescape Artificial Intelligence PvP Bot

Hidden Markov model based macro for player-vs-player
combat in popular MMORPG, Old School Runescape

Player vs player combat is a core component of the popular MMORPG Old School Runescape which has had multiple tournaments with cash prizes of $10,000-$20,000 each. Many players have played for years and are extremely skilled both in strategy and reflexes, able to react to rapidly changing situations. A hidden markov model is used to model a large number of states representing various levels of resources available to the player and their opponents and outputs the best course of action.
