Ebay Webscraper

Python-powered webscraper to find products at the best price from trusted sellers

Ebay webscraper utilizing the powerful Selenium library available in Python. Enter a desired product and assign criteria such as price range, year, condition, and more. Can be used for finding particular desired items or used in scale as part of a business. Results are sorted based on customizable criteria including tradeoffs between seller reputation and price. Variables are set automatically but can be manually fine-tuned.
